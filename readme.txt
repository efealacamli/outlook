Outlook
===============
2D puzzle platform game made with Flixel. 
Sam is a crazy inventor and he needs to 
collect special stones for his secret project 
by travelling in the parallel universe.
  
HOW TO PLAY:
	
	Left-Right arrow keys: Move left-right
	Up arrow key: Jump
	X key: Travel in parallel universe
